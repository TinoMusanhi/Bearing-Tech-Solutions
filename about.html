Company Overview

BEARING TECH SOLUTIONS (BTS) PVT LTD is a leading supplier of industrial components and engineering solutions based in Aspindale, Harare, Zimbabwe.

We are committed to providing our clients with exceptional service, top-tier products, and expert technical support. Our extensive industry knowledge allows us to supply high-performance solutions that improve operational efficiency, reduce downtime, and support sustainable industrial growth.

Whether you need bearings, power transmission components, seals, conveyor systems, motors, or engineering supplies — BTS ensures reliable delivery, ethical sourcing, and unmatched professional service.
